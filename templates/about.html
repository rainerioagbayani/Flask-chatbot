About OpenUP

The AI Chatbot named OpenUP has a decision support system to process making choices among various alternatives, and as a prototyping investigative system for proposing and answering a series of 'what if' scenarios.

The system aims to develop a chatbot that could aid and give resources to LGBT community victims who are not able to talk to someone within their circle. Our motivation is the desire to provide a place where victims of LGBT community can air out their minds in all safety and without being judged because some victims do not have anyone in whom they can confide.
